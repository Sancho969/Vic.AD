Biography

Victor Dada is a Nigerian-born music and computing student. He started playing the piano at 7 and later discovered the trumpet. He attended Calvary International School for primary education and Preston International School for secondary, earning a preliminary grade in Muson.

He was a member of the school choir and brass band. After secondary education, he studied graphic design (Photoshop, Illustrator, CorelDRAW). Later, he moved to the UK, completed BTEC Level 3 Extended Certificate & National Diploma in Computing, and currently studies Computer Science at the University of Derby.

Victor is passionate about music and performed pieces like "O Thou That Tellest" and "America Patrol".
